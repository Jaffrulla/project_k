Our Website Critic Foodie can aid you in finding about the restaurants at you give an idea about how others feel about those restaurants. We aim to provide all the reviews and ratings of the restaurants given by our Users. Any of our users can access reviews of all available restaurants and submit their own reviews and ratings out of 5 for any of the dishes. Users can also review the entire restaurant and submit their opinions about any of the other features of the restaurant like 'Hospitality', 'Ambiance', 'Vehicle Parking', 'Location availability', 'Timings' etc.
